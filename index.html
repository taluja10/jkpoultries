Our Story

A Farm Built on
Passion & Purpose

JK Poultries was founded in 1998 by the Krishnamurthy family in the fertile plains of Andhra Pradesh. What began as a small backyard flock of 50 hens has grown into one of the region's most respected free-range egg farms.

We believe that happy hens lay better eggs. Our birds roam on 40 acres of lush pasture, eating a natural diet of grasses, insects, and supplementary grain — never antibiotics, never artificial hormones.

Farm to Table

Our Journey

From sunrise on the farm to your breakfast plate — every step handled with care.

🐓

Pasture Life

Our hens spend their days outdoors on open pasture — foraging insects, dust-bathing, and living stress-free. Happy hens lay the best eggs.

🥚

Daily Collection

Eggs are hand-collected twice daily at dawn and dusk to ensure maximum freshness. Each nest box is checked and cleaned every morning.

🔬

Quality Grading

Every egg is candled, washed with food-safe solution, and graded by weight and shell quality. Cracked or misshapen eggs never reach customers.

📦

Eco Packaging

Packed in 100% recycled pulp trays printed with soy ink. We've eliminated all plastic from our supply chain since 2020.

🚐

Fresh Delivery

Our refrigerated vans deliver directly to your door within 24 hours of collection. No middlemen. No cold storage delays.
